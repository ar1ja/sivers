“Timely text”?

The big reason for this project is to find a bit of text on their page that should change when the page changes. Ideally it’s a date like “Updated September 22, 2025”, but could be “It’s currently winter 2025 now” or anything likely to change.

Copy that bit of text from their page, exactly as-is, and paste it into the “Timely text?” form field.

Then an automated script can look for that text every night, and when it’s not found, flag the page as probably changed.
